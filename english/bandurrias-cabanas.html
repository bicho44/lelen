Balcony Bandurrias

From San Martin de los Andes, going to Junin de los Andes, in the first roundabout turn left, take Route 48 to the turnoff to Bandurrias.

 

Once we arrive to the Mirador Bandurrias can visit two vantage points: first, we can admire the city of San Martin de los Andes from above, Pier and Chapelco.

The second will give us the ability to visualize: the Cerro Abanico, Catritre Beaches, Lake Lácar and Quila Quina.

 

If fatigue is not much and the desire to keep road still with us ... I recommend following up La Islita ... the reward will be wonderful! We only take half hour walk.

 

This is a white sand beach where you can relax, unwind and to have the opportunity to swim a short stretch of lake to get to the "little island."

Lake Lacar

The Lácar is a lake of glacial origin in the Pacific slope of Argentina, located in the province of Neuquén and part of the upper basin of the river Valdivia Chile.

Lanin Volcano

Lanin Volcano (3,776 m) is located in the south west of the Province of Neuquén (39 S, 71 O), near Junin de los Andes, on the banks of the provincial route 60 which links Argentina with Chile
