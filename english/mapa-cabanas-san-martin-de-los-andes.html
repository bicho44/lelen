How to get

By Plane

Traveling by plane to San Martin de los Andes: In high tourist season, flights from the federal capital and regional cities arrive daily at the local airport Airman Carlos Campos, located 22 kilometers from the town.

In Bus

Travelling by bus to San Martin de los Andes: The bus terminal, lakefront Lácar, 3 blocks Lelen Camping and five blocks from civic center, receives buses from around the country, and concentrated to companies connected to the city ​​with the main tourist routes of the region.

By Car

Traveling by Car in San Martin de los Andes: For visitors arriving by car into the region, an extensive network of national and provincial roads connecting San Martin de los Andes with the rest of the country, Chile, and with the cities of Junín de the Andes, Villa la Angostura and Bariloche.

The City

The city of San Martin de los Andes

It is located south of the province of Neuquén, approximately 430 kilometers from the provincial capital and a Federal Capital 1575.

 

Nestled in a broad valley on the shore of Lake Lácar, at a height of 640 meters above sea level, its privileged location makes it the gateway of the called Corridor of Lagos, an exceptional integrated circuit volcanoes, mirrors water, meltwater streams, rivers and forests.

 

The town sits on a natural system known as Wet Patagonian Andes, a set of mountains covered with dense forests native Nothofagus (false beech) such as oak pellín, coihue, lenga and ñire raulí, complemented with conifers such as cypress and pine, and a rich biodiversity of flora and fauna, protected by the National Park Lanin.

 

The immediate proximity to one of the largest conservation areas in Argentina, plus the urban vocation mountain village of the city, is an environmental quality region where issues such as clean air and crystal clear waters are a welcome constant

Lake Lacar

The Lácar is a lake of glacial origin in the Pacific slope of Argentina, located in the province of Neuquén and part of the upper basin of the river Valdivia Chile.
